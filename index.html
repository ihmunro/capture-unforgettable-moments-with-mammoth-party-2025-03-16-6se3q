About Mammoth Party

Founded in 2020, Mammoth Party emerged from a simple vision: to revolutionize how people capture and share moments at events. Our founders, experienced event photographers and tech innovators, recognized the need for a more engaging and immediate photo experience that could bring people together while creating lasting memories.

Today, we're driven by our mission to make every event extraordinary through innovative photo experiences. Our core values of creativity, reliability, and customer delight guide everything we do. Frequently Asked Questions

How does the instant photo delivery work?

Photos are automatically uploaded to our secure cloud platform and immediately available through a custom event gallery. Guests can access their photos via QR code or direct link, allowing for instant downloads and social media sharing.

What types of events do you service?

We cater to all types of events, including corporate gatherings, weddings, birthday parties, conferences, and promotional events. Our services are customizable to match any event size and theme.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for optimal availability, especially during peak seasons. However, we can sometimes accommodate last-minute bookings based on our schedule.

Can we customize the photo overlays?

Yes! We work with you to create custom branded overlays that match your event theme or company branding. Our design team will provide mockups for approval before the event.

What is included in the basic package?

Our basic package includes a professional host/photographer, unlimited photos, instant digital delivery, basic overlays, and a dedicated online gallery for 30 days. Additional services like custom branding and extended gallery access are available as add-ons.
