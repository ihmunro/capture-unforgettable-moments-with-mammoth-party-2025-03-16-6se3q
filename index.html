Founded in 2020, Mammoth Party emerged from a simple vision: to revolutionize how people capture and share moments at events. Our founders, experienced event photographers and tech innovators, recognized the need for a more engaging and immediate photo experience that could bring people together while creating lasting memories.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the instant photo delivery work?

Photos are automatically uploaded to our secure cloud platform and immediately available through a custom event gallery. Guests can access their photos via QR code or direct link, allowing for instant downloads and social media sharing. The entire process takes just seconds from capture to delivery.

What types of events do you service?

We cater to all types of events, including corporate gatherings, weddings, birthday parties, conferences, promotional events, holiday celebrations, graduation parties, reunions, and more. Our services are customizable to match any event size and theme.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for optimal availability, especially during peak seasons (May-September and December). However, we can sometimes accommodate last-minute bookings based on our schedule. For large corporate events or weddings during peak season, booking 2-3 months in advance is advisable.

Can we customize the photo overlays?

Yes! We work with you to create custom branded overlays that match your event theme or company branding. Our design team will provide mockups for approval before the event. We can incorporate logos, event hashtags, color schemes, and decorative elements that align with your vision and aesthetic preferences.

What is included in the basic package?

Our basic package includes a professional host/photographer, unlimited photos, instant digital delivery, basic overlays, a standard set of props, and a dedicated online gallery for 30 days. Additional services like custom branding, extended gallery access, printed photos, and premium backdrops are available as add-ons or in our higher-tier packages.
